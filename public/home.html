The job to be done

Stop practising on real commercial buyers.

Big jobs are won in awkward, high-leverage moments: getting past the gatekeeper, earning permission, explaining relevance, qualifying budget or energy spend, routing procurement, and exiting cleanly when there is no fit.

  1. Pick the commercial moment Gatekeeper, finance, facilities, procurement, hard no, or follow-up.
  2. Run the AI roleplay The customer pushes back, interrupts, asks for proof, or routes you elsewhere.
  3. Review the weak moment See the scored skill, suggested wording, and the next drill to repeat.

Deliberate practice loop

Coach the next move, not just the final score.

When you ask for Help, the trainer uses the current stage and objection to ask you to choose a move before revealing a suggested line. Weak skills return as spaced-repetition drills until the behaviour holds under pressure.

Why open source

Transparent playbooks contractors can actually adapt.

Many sales-training tools are built for broad revenue teams. This project is designed to be inspected, self-hosted, forked, and customised by contractors, agencies, coaches, and trade communities.

  • Run locally or on your own server.
  • Edit scenarios, scoring rubrics, and approved lines.
  • Add trade packs without changing the core app.
  • Use mock mode for fully local practice.
  • Connect OpenClaw or another provider only when transcript context can be shared with that provider.
  • Keep sensitive training transcripts under your control when self-hosting.
